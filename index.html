Cherry Creek Trail

Bike the Cherry Creek Bike Trail. The Cherry Creek Regional Trail is a picturesque 40-mile route that begins in downtown Denver.

Cherry Creek Reservoir

Swim or kayak in the Cherry Creek Reservoir. A scenic oasis in the Denver area offering a wide variety of water and land activities for outdoor enthusiasts.

88 Drive In Movie Theater

Catch a movie at the drive in movie theater. Enjoy the movie theater experience while staying safe. This theater has been open since 1972.



Rocky Mountain National Park

With majestic mountains, abundant wildlife, Trail Ridge Road (the highest paved road in the US), and over 350 miles of trails, Rocky Mountain National Park is spectacular!

Washington Park

Grab some sandwiches and beverages, and enjoy a picnic at Washington Park with some friends. Make sure to stay 6 feet apart.

Ski Resorts

From Breckenridge to Loveland to Crested Butte, Colorado has some of the best skiing and snowboarding runs in the world. And everyone is already wearing gloves and masks!
